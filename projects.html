Goals and Projects

We innovate by continually learning from existing platforms as a way to produce novel approaches to global issues, including climate change. We approach these challenges by drawing from engineering, chemistry, and physical fields in order to produce regenerative agricultural templates as an initial approach to rewrite the ways that we interact with our surrounding world.

Currently, I am conducting a year-long field study through a trans-Americas expedition by bicycle. Traveling by bike allows is a sustainable approach to visit different agricultural sites and farmers to research conventional and alternative methodologies. To see more about the biking adventure, you can follow our journey here.

My aim is to collect real-time environmental data and samples for analysis towards designing 1) an autonomous robotic instrument and 2) generate crop templates based on our collected data sets.
