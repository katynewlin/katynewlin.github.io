We innovate by continually learning from existing platforms as a way to produce novel approaches to global issues, including climate change. We approach these challenges by drawing from engineering, chemistry, and physical fields in order to produce regenerative agricultural templates as an initial approach to rewrite the ways that we interact with our surrounding world.

Currently, we are on an expedition by bike from Boston to Kingston. We are traversing Canada with the intent to travel sustainably to different farms to research their current approaches. Follow our journey here.

We are traveling with the intent to collect data in real-time to design 1) a robotic prototype that will operate autonomously on farms globally and 2) generating agricultural layouts based on our collected data sets.
