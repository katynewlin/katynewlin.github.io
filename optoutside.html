Katy Newlin's Opt Outside

Deciding to #optoutside and explore is truly a magical and powerful medicine that we can all access. Whether we are soaking in the sun streaming in through out windows to backpacking through Patagonia, becoming aware and curious about the world around us is incredible. This podcast is designed to get everyone outside to enjoy the world around us! Join us, listen, and Opt Outside!
