Newlin Research

engineering feats to shift our world

Alternatives for global challenges

through collaborative and research-based approaches

Our research focuses on elucidating molecular interactions of natural phenomena, specifically towards agricultural methodologies, and used our findings to generate solutions where there are seemingly none. We explore and develop long-term approaches for sustainable living in our modern world.

We innovate by continually learning from existing platforms as a way to produce novel approaches to global issues. We approach these challenges by drawing from engineering, chemistry, and physical fields in order to generate regenerative agricultural templates to rewrite how we interact with our surrounding world.
