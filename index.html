Traveling the Americas

redefining the way we live

Exploring observing connecting

to redefine the way we live for humanity and our world

I am motivated to alter the ways we live due to the most pressing issues of our time: inequality and subsequently, global climate change. Traveling has become a way for me to integrate with local communities and countries at large, to experience the existing relationships between people and the land. By living among various social and industrial infrastructures, I experience how and why our world works currently. I aim to share my encounters of social constructs and natural phenomena to bring awareness to the ways we live for a better today.
