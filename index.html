to redefine the way we live for humanity and our world via science and movement.

I provide access to education through science and movement. I am motivated to alter the ways we live due to the most pressing issues of our time: inequality and subsequently, global climate change. I explore as a way to integrate with local communities and countries, to experience the existing relationships between people and the land. By living among various social and industrial infrastructures, I am aware of the ever increasing barriers to accessing education so I created this community for all of us to thrive.
