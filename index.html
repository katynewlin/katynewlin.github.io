Newlin Research

engineering feats to shift our world

Exploring sustainable alternatives

for global challenges through collaborative and research-based approaches

Our research focuses on elucidating molecular interactions that govern natural phenomena. We employ our findings to generate solutions where there are seemingly none. We are currently targetting the realm of climate change - an immediate global demand - with a focus on redesigning conventional agriculture methodologies. Here, we explore and develop long-term approaches for sustainable living in our modern world by learning from existing concepts and natural forces that underpin the way our world works.
